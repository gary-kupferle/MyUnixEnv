iOS Notes (for iPad and iPhone)

Deleting all downloaded music
- may need to do this first and reverse it when finished
  * Settings...iTunes & App Store
  * turn off "SHOW ALL"..Music and "iTunes Match"
- Settings...General...Usage...Manage Storage...Music
- press "Edit"
- press red delete circle in front of "All Songs"

Find My iPhone/iPad
- browse icloud.com
- press "Find My iPhone" button
