iDVD Notes

Here are the steps for one way to create a DVD.
1) Start iDVD
2) Select "iMagic DVD"
3) Enter a title.
4) Select a theme.
4) Drag movies from Finder to the appropriate boxes.
5) Drag photos, if any, from Finder to the appropriate boxes.
6) Press the "Create Project" button.
7) Press the burn button (twice?) that is
   just to the right of the triangle play button.
8) When prompted, insert a blank DVD.
9) Wait a long time for the encoding and burning process to finish.
10) The DVD will be ejected automatically.
