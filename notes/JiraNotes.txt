# Jira notes

## To add an estimate to an issue
- it must be in the backlog
- click the issue in the backlog
- in the right pane, click to the right of "Estimate"
  and enter a number

## To create a new sprint
- scroll to the top of the backlog
- press the "Create Sprint" button in the upper-right

## To end a sprint
- switch to the Kanban view which is the
  second view in the vertial list on the left
- click the "Complete Sprint" link in the upper-right
